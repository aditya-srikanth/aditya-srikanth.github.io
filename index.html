Aditya Srikanth Veerubhotla

I am a recent graduate at LTI, CMU where I pursued my master's in Intelligent Information Systems - Advanced Study. Through my projects and TA work, I got the opportunity to work with a lot of people, including Prof Jamie Callan, Prof Eric Nyberg, Prof Graham Neubig, Prof Yonatan Bisk, Prof Daniel Fried, and my awesome peers at CMU!

Over the past few years, I got to do research on a lot of interesting problems in NLP covering Aspect Based Sentiment Analysis, Question Answering/Generation, Information Retrieval, Multimodal Machine Learning, Self-Rationalization, and more.

I have prior work experience as an Applied Scientist Microsoft Bing. I have also interned at Amazon, Microsoft, and at the L.V. Prasad Eye Institute in a collaborative program with MIT Media Labs.
